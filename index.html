About us:

Goffee originated from the words "Go" and "Coffee" and was founded by its owner, Farload Barcena, in 2023. It began as a small motor cart that roamed around various areas in Camarines Norte, but through hard work and perseverance, it grew into its own shop within just a year. It also became well-known for the owner‚Äôs friendly and excellent treatment of customers, many of whom eventually became loyal patrons. That's why GOffee is an ideal spot not only for students but also for couples and families who truly enjoy coffee. In addition to their coffee offerings, GOffee also serves matcha and cocktail drinks. The shop takes pride in using high-quality, imported ingredients to enhance the flavor of their beverages. Customers can also have fun playing a variety of board games while sipping on their favorite drinks.
